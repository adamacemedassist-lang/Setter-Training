Closer Training & Selection Terms
Please read carefully before proceeding with your 5-day closer training.
Unpaid Training: The 5-day training period is completely unpaid. You will be given targets during training. Completing them is mandatory to be considered for the Closer role.
Closer Challenge: After training you must complete 15 appts in 5 days with 1 closing by another closer (Day 6–10), then another 15 appts with 5 closings yourself (Day 11–17). Elite closers finish in 5 days, average in 10–15.
Commission Plan: You receive commission based on your closing performance. Full structure shared during orientation.
Attendance: Remain active in the workspace during training. If unavailable, inform on WhatsApp: 03197874033 (Yousaf – Manager). Failure to inform may result in removal.
Feedback: Your feedback helps us improve. Please share at: feedback form →
